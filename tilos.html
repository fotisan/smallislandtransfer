Your Route from Rhodes to Tilos

1. Pickup at your point

We collect you from Rhodes Airport, your hotel or any agreed location, with enough lead time built in for a stress-free connection.

2. Direct to the ferry port

We drive you straight to Rhodes Old Town port, the departure point for all Tilos ferries — no detours, no guesswork about which terminal.

3. Cross to Tilos

The catamaran takes around 1h45min, the ferry around 2h30min. You board relaxed, knowing the logistics are behind you.

4. Arrive in Livadia

You step off the ferry into one of the quietest, most unspoiled corners of the Dodecanese — ready to disconnect and explore at your own pace.
